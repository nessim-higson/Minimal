A quiet site that can say more.

The premise: Marvin Schwaibold's restraint — one small centered column, work as big cards, a sheet that lifts to reveal the footer — crossed with nternet.company's less/more dial, where the intro is one sentence that grows. 04 · ASK THE SITE — STORYBOARD

The site answers with itself

No chat window, no bubbles. A question is just another way to navigate: the page scrolls, expands the dial, and highlights the answer in situ.

1 · ASK — "what's your background?"
typed into the dock, or ⌘K
2 · THE SITE MOVES — dial auto-cranks to L3, scrolls to intro, marker-highlights the relevant words
3 · ANSWER STRIP — one italic line above the dock confirms; fades after a beat. "Show me WKNDR" → scrolls + pulses the card instead
v1 ships as keyword routing (zero backend, instant) · v2 swaps in a real model over the same actions — the interaction contract doesn't change
